instructions
  • Regularly inspect the bed for any loose parts, wear, or damage. Ensure all connections are secure and the bed is stable before use
  • Vacuum or wipe the bed frame with a damp cloth to remove dust and dirt. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners that could damage the product’s finish
  • Remove the mattress and any bedding before cleaning. Wash the mattress cover and bed sheets according to the manufacturer's instructions
  • Spot-clean the mattress with a mild soap and water solution if needed. Allow it to air dry completely before placing it back in the bed.
  • Clean the fabric surfaces with a damp cloth and a mild soap solution. Gently scrub any dirt or stains, rinse with clean water, and let it air dry
  • Store the bed in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent any damage or mold growth
  • Follow the manufacturer's weight and age limit guidelines to ensure the bed remains safe and suitable for your baby
